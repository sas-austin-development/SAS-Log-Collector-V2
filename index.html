Please follow the Below Steps to Submit Deployment Logs

Step 1: Go to your C Drive

Please open up your C Drive. This can be done a few ways:

  1. Open your File Explorer, and go to the location shown in the image above
  2. In your search bar, search "C:"

Step 2: Double Click on Users Folder

While in your C: Drive, double click your Users folder.


Step 3: Double Click on UserID who Installed SAS

Click on the UserId of the User who installed SAS. In most cases, this will be your UserId. If you did not install SAS, please contact your Administrator or IT to continue.


Step 4: Double Click on AppData

Double Click on the AppData folder.

Note: The AppData directory is a hidden folder.

For assistance enabling the display of hidden files and folders in Windows, see Show hidden files.


Step 5: Double Click on Local

Double click on the Local Folder.


Step 6: Double click on SAS

Double click on the SAS Folder.


Step 7: Create a SAS Deployment Wizard Zipped Folder

To create a SAS Deployment Wizard Zipped Folder, please do the following:

  1. Right click on the SAS Deployment Wizard Folder.
  2. Hover your mouse pointer over Send toas highlighted above.
  3. Click on Compressed (zipped) folder Folder.
  4. Give your Zipped Folder a name and save it to a location you will remember.

Step 8 - Verify the Zip File was Created

Verify that the Zipped File was created by looking at the Type of the file.
